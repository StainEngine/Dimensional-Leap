# Dimensional-Leap

Dimensional Leap game repository.


Dimensional Leap is a game that will be added to Steam in the future, and made by a team of people.


Premise: Get ready for an unforgettable adventure in this 3D game where anything is possible!

Stumbling through an unknown portal, you find yourself falling into a crazy dimension full of unique creatures big and small, where nothing seems to make sense.

After meeting an excited vacuum named Vurmy who needs your help, travel across multiple dimensions, meet the locals, and take on many crazy quests, as you find yourself entangled in stopping an evil frogs plans to take over all the dimensions.

Take on a dance battle, destroy building using a wrecking ball made from NPC's, become two dimensional and more while using Vurmy's many abilities to save the day.


The team consists of a few people, and Gavin/Froggy is the lead behind the game.


(Edit: This game has been shelved, and is archived for preservation purposes. Gavin might return to the game later and work on the first area, for fun...)
