# Dimensional-Leap

Dimensional Leap game repository.


Dimensional Leap is a game by a team that will be added to Steam in the future.

(Edit: This game has been shelved, and is archived for preservation purposes. Gavin might return to the game later and work on the first area, for fun...)


Premise: Get ready for an unforgettable adventure in this 3D game where anything is possible!

Stumbling through an unknown portal, you find yourself falling into a crazy dimension full of unique creatures big and small, and an excuted vacuum named Vurmy who needs your help. With Vurmy by your side, travel across multiple dimensions using your frog ship, meet the locals, and take on quests!

Take on a dance battle, destroy building using a wrecking ball made from NPC's, become two dimensional, and stop an evil frog from taking over all the dimensions for his ultimate plans, while using Vurmy's many abilities to save the day.


The team consists of a few people, and Gavin/Froggy is the lead behind the game.
